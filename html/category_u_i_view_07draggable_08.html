- (CGRect) cagingArea
readwritenonatomicassign

A caging area such that the view can not be moved outside of this frame.

If cagingArea is not CGRectZero, and cagingArea does not contain the view's frame then this does nothing (ie. if the bounds of the view extend the bounds of cagingArea).

Optional. If not set, defaults to CGRectZero, which will result in no caging behavior.
